Current Relief Efforts: California Wildfires

We are currently focused on providing critical support to California communities affected by devastating wildfires. Your tax-deductible contribution helps us deliver immediate relief and long-term recovery assistance, including:

  • Emergency shelter and supplies

  • Medical assistance
  • Long-term rebuilding support

Every donation makes a difference in helping communities recover and rebuild.

Matching Gifts: Double the Impact of Your Donation

Your gift to the C.V. Starr Disaster Relief Fund could be matched dollar for dollar by your employer! Many employers sponsor matching gift programs and will match any charitable contributions or volunteer hours made by their employees, retirees and/or employees' spouses.

How to Request a Matching Gift From Your Employer

If your company matches donations made to the C.V. Starr Disaster Relief Fund, here's how you can request your matching gift contribution:

1. If your company uses an online matching gift portal

Use your company's matching gift platform to submit your matching gift verification request.

Please note: the C.V. Starr Disaster Relief Fund EIN is: 81-4728239

2. If your company uses printed forms

Obtain a matching gift verification form from your company's personnel/human resources office. Complete the form using your company's official name. Be sure to complete the entire donor portion of the form and to include a copy of your gift receipt (if available). If you would like to designate the matching funds to a specific disaster, please indicate that on the form.

If you made your donation through C.V. Starr Disaster Relief Fund in person or starrdrf.org, please email (preferred) or mail the form and a copy of your receipt to:

Email: programstaff@starrfoundation.org
